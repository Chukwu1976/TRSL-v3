Cantele Telero Puglia IGT Rosso

The Cantele Telero Puglia IGT Rosso is a great value red wine blend of the local Negroamaro (80%) grape alongside the international varieties of Cabernet Sauvignon and Merlot. It is a delicate, fruity wine with soft tones of dark berries: sour cherries, black currants, plums and caraway seeds. On the palate, the wine is full-bodied, with a good balance and soft, velvety tannins. The finish is fruity and soft. Telero Rosso serves perfectly with traditional pasta and pizza dishes, light meats, cold snacks and various bruschettes.
